expander()[source]¶

Method designed to treat those entities that are compounds of two words which are identified as word1-word2. These compond words can be written in multiple forms: “word1-word2”, “word1word2”, “word1 word2”. Here, these forms are generated for each identified compund word (when ‘-’ is identified in the entity)
